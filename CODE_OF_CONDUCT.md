# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion constructive and
on-topic. Harassment, discrimination, and abuse are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **contact@ionalpha.io**. Reports are handled
confidentially. Maintainers may remove content, and may warn, suspend, or ban
contributors who violate this code.
